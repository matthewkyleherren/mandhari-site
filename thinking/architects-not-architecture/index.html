Architects not Architecture

Find out how communes, community and intergenerational living have shaped Sadie's outlook and professional life

Sadie Morgan - dRMM
by Architects, not Architecture

Director

Footage of Sadie speaking at the first UK edition of Architects, not Architecture.

The Architects, not Architecture event series provides a glimpse on great personalities of architecture. The event series is not about the projects of the architects, but about them, their professional backgrounds and formative individual experiences that shaped the projects we all know.
